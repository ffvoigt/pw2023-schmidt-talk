Schmidt Objective: Theoretical Performance

Schmidt Objective Prototype

  • Optimized for multiphoton imaging
    • Ti:Sa Laser: 100 fs & 750-1000 nm
  • NA ∝ n
  • NA · FOV = const.
  • FOV ∝ 1/n
  • Diffraction-limited FOV (Ø) of the design:
    • 1.7 mm in air (n=1.00)
    • 1.4 mm in water (n=1.33)
    • 1.1 mm in ECI (n=1.56)
  • Curved focal surface: r ≈ 15 mm
    • @ 400 µm FOV-Ø: Δz ≈ 2 um
    • Not a problem in cleared samples
  • Working distance: ≈11 mm
  • Any homogeneous medium works!
  • The sample partially blocks the optical path
    • Usable up to samples of 6 x 6 mm
